› PURCHASE

Purchasing LAR 01 is the most complete and apparent way to own an
aircraft. It is especially popular among corporate clientele,

›LEASING

A form of usage attractive to companies that do not want to or are
not able to purchase a jet. Leasing allows using the LAR 01

to acquire the aircraft and include it into the assets of your
company.
